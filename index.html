The above is a treemap of treemaps of the S&P 500 Companies by Sector, created using calls to the Finnhub API. After linking is completed, you'll be able to select one of the treemaps and that visualization will zoom in to display a tremap for just that sector. Next, a user can select two of the companies to display in the Parallel Coordinates Graph and the Differences Chart, shown below the Treemaps. Two of the companies' information about market capitalization, CEO salary, total compensation, current price, and number of employees will be displayed on the Parallel Coordinates chart. The Difference chart will compare the differences in two stocks time series data after getting two companies.
